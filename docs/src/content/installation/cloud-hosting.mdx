# Cloud Hosting Setup

Get started with our hosted analytics service in under 60 seconds. No infrastructure management required!

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. **Visit** [betterlytics.io/signup](https://betterlytics.io/signup)
2. **Sign up** with your email address
3. **Complete** your profile setup

## Step 2: Create Your Website Dashboard

1. **Click** "Create Dashboard" in your account overview
2. **Enter** your domain name (e.g., `example.com`)

3. **Choose your plan:**
   - **Free Plan:** Up to 10,000 page views/month - perfect for small sites
   - **Dynamic Pricing:** 10,001+ page views - pay-as-you-scale with our pricing slider
   - **Enterprise:** 10M+ page views/month - [contact us](mailto:support@betterlytics.io) for custom pricing

Your dashboard will be created instantly with a unique tracking ID.

## Step 3: Install Your Tracking Script

Once your dashboard is created, **click the "Integration" button** in your dashboard to access your personalized setup guide.

### What You'll Get:

- **Your unique Site ID** - automatically generated for your dashboard
- **Copy-ready tracking script** - personalized with your Site ID
- **Platform-specific examples** - HTML, Next.js, React, and more
- **Real-time verification** - test your installation instantly
- **Integration status tracking** - see your progress step-by-step

### Quick Overview:

You'll add a lightweight script (under 1KB) to your website's `<head>` section:

```html
<script
  async
  src="https://betterlytics.io/analytics.js"
  data-site-id="your-unique-site-id"
></script>
```

**Important:** Use your personalized integration guide in your dashboard - it contains your actual Site ID and provides copy-to-clipboard functionality for easy installation.

## Step 4: Verify Your Installation

Your dashboard's integration guide includes:

- **Real-time verification** - check if data is flowing
- **Integration status tracker** - see exactly what's working

Data typically appears within 1-2 minutes of installation.
